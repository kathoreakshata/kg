latex gloves

Latex gloves are made of Natural rubber, a natural origin material (from a rubber tree), Latex offers good resistance against many acids and alkalis. Latex gloves offer a high degree of protection against a broad range of hazards, including chemical and biohazards, and abrasive or tearing hazards. Latex provides a greater level of strength and protection against punctures and tears.

Nitrile Gloves

Nitrile is a synthetic copolymer, formed through acrylonitrile and butadiene combining. Nitrile Gloves also have superior chemical resistance to other disposable gloves. This is due to the fact that Nitrile Rubber has better resistance to chemicals, acids & oils over against natural rubber. Nitrile Gloves are also powder free, these further reduce the risk of allergens and dealing with messy powder.
