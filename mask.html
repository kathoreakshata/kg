face mask

In today's world, wearing a surgical mask has become a necessary practice to ensure personal and public safety.

With the ongoing pandemic, surgical masks have become a vital tool in reducing the spread of harmful airborne particles and droplets that can carry infectious diseases.

Surgical masks are designed to filter out large respiratory droplets and other particles that can transmit viruses and bacteria, providing a barrier between the wearer and the outside world.

3Ply surgical Face mask

The 3-ply surgical mask is a disposable mask that has 3 layers of non-woven fabric to help you breathe clean air. This is a special medical-grade face mask with higher protection than a civilian mask. It is one with 3 layers with the inner and outer layers being non-woven and the middle layer made from high-standard filter paper.
